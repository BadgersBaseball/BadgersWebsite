Where does my money go?

At Badgers Baseball we aim to provide competitive baseball at an affordable cost to families. With the rising cost of living and the extraordinary prices of some clubs, we wanted to be different. We ask a small player fee of $300. Our expenses far out way the collection of these fees. We feel we can achieve our goal through donations, fundraising and sponsorship. Expenses for Badgers Baseball include uniforms, practice field fees, tournament fees of around $500-$675 per tournament and expenses accrued in acquiring and maintaining our non profit status. Our coaches and support staff are all volunteer. Our goal is not to make money but instead to one day offer a free baseball program for those we serve. We kindly thank you if choose you to support our mission. Thank you, and Go Badgers!

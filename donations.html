Where does my money go?

Badgers Baseball was created to provide competitive baseball at an affordable price for families: The exorbitant fees of other local clubs made access for many gifted players impossible, and we want to be different.

Our players pay a yearly registration fee of $300, and we rely on donations, fundraising, and sponsorship to fund the rest of our expenses. Badgers Baseball expenses include uniforms, practice field fees, tournament fees (approx. $600 per tournament), fees related to maintaining non-profit status, etc.

The support staff and coaches for Badgers Baseball are all volunteers working collectively to provide a quality program to the youth that we serve: Our hope is that one day we will be able to offer full scholarships to families in need of the extra assistance.

Thank you for your consideration and for the support of our mission. Go Badgers!

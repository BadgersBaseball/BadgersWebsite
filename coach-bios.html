Our Coach

Kevin Owens

Kevin Owens is entering his 15th year in total coaching. Kevin has coached levels ranging from 8U to the Junior College Level. At the age of 19, he got his start during his college baseball days as an Assistant Summer Baseball Coach for Narrows Baseball Academy 18U. He was also an Assistant Coach in Augusta, GA for the Augusta Barons 18U during his latter years of college. He was a Volunteer Assistant in year 1 and in year 2 named Head Assistant Baseball Coach at Everett Community College between 2011 and 2012. During his two years he helped guide the Everett Trojans in back to back NWAAC Regional Tournament Appearances including a final 4 finish in 2012. In 2011 and 2012 both teams finished as Co-Champions of the NWAC North Division. During that time he was named the Academy hitting Instructor for Laces Baseball out of Everett running the offense for teams ranging from 13 to 18. After a 3 year coaching break where Kevin focussed on his soon to be family, he decided to get back into the coaching world being hired as Head Varsity Baseball Coach for Peninsula High School from 2016-2018. There are a handful of players from those years still active in college baseball today. Choosing to focus on family again after 2018, Kevin began working with his children Hudson and Wesley and their baseball lives. In 2020 Kevin and his children moved to Puyallup coaching Little League Baseball for which he's found more passion than he ever thought possible. Kevin looks forward to years of helping young kids grow their lives through the game of baseball. His goal is to give players a compeititive Travel Ball experience while making it affordable for families.

Player Credentials: During his playing career Kevin was named Varsity Team MVP 3 times and Gold Glove once at Gig Harbor High School. He was elected All-League Catcher and selected to the All-State Showcase Series in 2004. He was a member of the NWAC Academic All League in 2006 at Everett Community College. During that year he was able to compete in the NWAC Regional Tournament going 9 of 14 at the plate during the tourney. In 2008, Kevin was named All Peach Belt and All Southeast Conference Gold Glove out of Augusta State University. Kevin was able to gain a Bachelors in Science at Augusta State University in the Field of Health and Physical Education.

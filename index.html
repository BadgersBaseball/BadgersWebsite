Our Mission

Badgers Baseball was formed in 2022 after a group of parents and talented players decided they wanted to create a space to experience competitive baseball a little differently. Youth baseball is becoming a lucrative business in many ways, and Badgers Baseball would like to bring the focus back to the game and its players.

Badgers Baseball wants kids to be able to play any sport they want to without having to commit to only one path; we believe a well rounded athlete who has both physical and mental strength will result from this opportunity.

Badgers Baseball strives to provide more than just baseball drills and a winning team: Our goal is to teach our players how to compete, how to stand up to adversity successfully, and how to be a supportive teammate. Badgers Baseball is a nonprofit organization.

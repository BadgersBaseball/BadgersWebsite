Badgers Baseball was formed in 2022 from a group of talented kids and parents looking to break away from what we saw becoming the “norm” in youth baseball. We saw that the youth game was becoming more and more a business than a game. Badgers Baseball is a 501 nonprofit. We saw a need for change, we saw a need to put the kids first, the total athlete first. Badgers Baseball wants kids to play every sport they want to, building a stronger athlete both physically and mentally. Our biggest aim is to provide more than skill set teaching. Teaching our players how to compete, how to handle adversity and building life character through sport.
